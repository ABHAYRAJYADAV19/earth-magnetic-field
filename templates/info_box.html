🌍 Earth's Magnetic Field

What is it?

E“The Earth’s magnetic field is like a giant invisible force field that surrounds our planet, created deep inside the Earth. Just like a magnet, it has a north and south pole. This field helps guide compasses, protects us from harmful solar radiation, and even makes the beautiful northern lights possible. It’s nature’s way of shielding life on Earth and helping us find our direction..

The Dynamo Effect

The field is generated by the movement of liquid iron in Earth's outer core, creating a self-sustaining magnetic dynamo.

Did you know?

The Aurora Borealis (Northern Lights) occurs when solar particles interact with Earth's magnetic field!
