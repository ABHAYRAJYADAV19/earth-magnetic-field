What is it?

Earth's magnetic field is a protective bubble that surrounds our planet, created by the movement of molten iron in Earth's core.

The Dynamo Effect

The field is generated by the movement of liquid iron in Earth's outer core, creating a self-sustaining magnetic dynamo.

Did you know?

The Aurora Borealis (Northern Lights) occurs when solar particles interact with Earth's magnetic field!
